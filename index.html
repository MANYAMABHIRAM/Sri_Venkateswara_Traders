About Us

Welcome to Sri Venkateswara Traders, your trusted partner in providing top-quality construction materials, agricultural products, and electrical/plumbing equipment. Nestled in the heart of G.Sigadam near BPCL Petrol Bunk, our establishment is a beacon for those seeking excellence in building materials and agricultural supplies.

At Sri Venkateswara Traders, we are committed to delivering a diverse range of products to cater to your specific needs. Whether you are embarking on a construction project, tending to your agricultural endeavors, or in need of electrical and plumbing solutions, we have you covered.

Our founder, P. Naidu, has envisioned a one-stop destination that not only provides high-quality products but also values customer satisfaction. With a strong foundation built on trust, integrity, and reliability, we take pride in being an integral part of the community we serve.

What sets us apart is our dedication to offering a comprehensive product catalog. From Deccan Cement's PPC(Graded) & OPC(Grade Less) to the finest iron sourced from Vishaka Steel and Simhadri Steel, our commitment to quality is unwavering. Additionally, our Electricals & Plumbing section features products from renowned companies like GOLD MEDAL and ND9, ensuring reliability and safety for your projects.

In the realm of agriculture, we provide a wide array of fertilizers including UREA, DAP, Gromore, and meticulously selected seeds such as Corn (Advanta & Syngenta), Vegetable Seeds, and more. Sri Venkateswara Traders is not just a store; it's a partner in your growth and success.
